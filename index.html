Straight answers

Does my parent get a new phone number?

No. Their number stays exactly the same, and so does their phone. SafeDial works through call forwarding on their existing line.

Does my parent have to learn anything new?

No. Calls ring and answer on the same screen they already know. SafeDial was designed so that the person it protects never has to operate it.

What phones does it work with?

iPhone and Android. During setup we also confirm their phone plan supports call forwarding - most current Canadian postpaid plans include it, and we check yours before anything changes.

Is someone listening to my parent's calls?

Software screens live calls for scam patterns - not people. Ordinary calls are discarded after they end. Only calls flagged as scams are kept for your review, and you can delete those too.

What if a real call gets flagged by mistake?

SafeDial only ends a call on high-confidence signs of a scam. Anything merely odd continues normally while you get a quiet heads-up, and every decision comes with its reasoning so you can correct the trusted-caller list in one tap.
